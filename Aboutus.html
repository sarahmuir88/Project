Sarah and Bronagh began their coding journey with Code First Girls. Lonely and scared of the vast coding world ahead, they decided to join forces and create a safe, coffee-filled space where they could meet up with other tech-hungry people. Code Café was born. What began on one Saturday afternoon in south London is now a global phenomenon with particpating cafés in over 100 countries around the world.
